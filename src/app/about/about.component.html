    Information about Mirchi Yard

    "Mirchi Yard" is a common term used in India to refer to a marketplace or yard where chili peppers (also known as "mirchi" in Hindi) are bought and sold. These yards are usually major trading centers for chili peppers, and farmers, wholesalers, retailers, and exporters come together to carry out the trade of this popular spice.Andhra Pradesh, particularly the Guntur district, is famous for its chili pepper production and is home to one of the largest chili markets in Asia, the "Guntur Mirchi Yard." This market is known for its vibrant trading activity and plays a significant role in the chili pepper industry.
